# Artami Motion Graphics Plan

**Status:** Brainstorming in progress

This is the shared working brief for Artami's launch motion graphic. It records decisions confirmed during brainstorming; it is not the final storyboard or implementation specification.

## Goal

Create a motion-graphics campaign that drives Android app downloads after Artami is approved and published on Google Play. The video should make Artami feel premium, clear, trustworthy, and recognizably connected to the product UI and landing-page brand.

## Audience

- Broad Indonesian audience, primarily ages 25-35.
- Relevant to salaried workers, freelancers, students, and young families.
- Language is Bahasa Indonesia with straightforward, relatable finance language.
- The fictional scenario follows a young salaried professional: predictable monthly income, everyday jajan/commute/tagihan spending, and an aspiration to build savings and financial freedom.
- The scenario shows a monthly salary of `Rp 5.000.000`; income is visible so the expense breakdown, savings rate, and cash-flow forecast have clear context.
- The month begins slightly pressured but recoverable: approximately `Rp 4.600.000` in expenses and `Rp 400.000` remaining for savings. This makes the need for clarity, planning, and projected improvement believable.
- The forecast shows a gradual recovery, not a warning-only outcome: after identifying flexible spending, monthly savings rise from `Rp 400.000` to a practical `Rp 600.000` target. This represents an attainable savings-rate improvement from 8% to 12%, rather than a dramatic lifestyle change.
- The illustrated starting Health Score is `54/100`: it signals a clear but recoverable need for attention and aligns with the slightly pressured cash-flow scenario.
- Display Health Score as an honest projected improvement: `54 sekarang -> target 68`, not as an already achieved result.
- The illustrated FI Index is `12%` toward financial independence, presenting realistic early-stage progress for a young salaried professional.
- The top-expense mix represents a modern young professional: `Kos & Tagihan`, `Makan & Minum`, `Transportasi`, `Langganan`, and `Jajan`.
- The finalized illustrative expense split totals `Rp 4.600.000`:
  - `Kos & Tagihan`: `Rp 1.840.000` (40%)
  - `Makan & Minum`: `Rp 920.000` (20%)
  - `Transportasi`: `Rp 368.000` (8%)
  - `Jajan`: `Rp 552.000` (12%)
  - `Langganan`: `Rp 920.000` (20%, the remaining amount)

## Campaign Use

- Primary master: 25-30 second social marketing video.
- Landscape-first master at `1920 x 1080` for landing-page, YouTube, presentation, and general launch use.
- Provide an optional `1280 x 720` export profile when a publishing platform limits resolution.
- Create a separately recomposed vertical cutdown later for TikTok, Instagram Reels, and YouTube Shorts; do not simply crop the landscape master.
- The master can later provide shorter social cutdowns and a product-led Play Store preview.

## Conversion Goal

- Only CTA: **Download Artami**.
- Final frame includes the Artami icon and official Google Play badge.
- Do not include pricing, lifetime-access messaging, QR codes, or APK-download language in this motion graphic.
- Publish only after the Google Play listing is approved and live.

## Creative Direction

- Product-demo-led direct-response structure: fast, relatable hook followed by clear product proof.
- Motion personality: smooth and premium, not fast or aggressive.
- Use Artami's warm cream, earth, violet, moss, clay, and amber palette.
- Preserve Artami's rounded bento cards, soft mesh gradients, subtle glass treatment, editorial serif accents, and clean Indonesian UI styling.
- No people, lifestyle footage, avatars, or AI-generated realistic video.
- No voiceover. Story is carried by Indonesian kinetic text, product motion, and sound effects.

## Story Arc

1. **Confusion:** income arrives but money feels like it disappears.
2. **Clarity:** Artami organizes transactions into useful statistics.
3. **Intelligence:** Health Score, FI Index, and financial forecast turn data into direction.
4. **Ownership:** data remains in the user's own Google Sheets, not Artami's server.
5. **Proof:** a short real Artami app capture demonstrates that the product is real.
6. **Action:** Artami icon, `Download Artami`, and Google Play badge.

## Selected Storytelling Approach

**Guided Financial Journey** is the approved structure. The video is one connected progression rather than a feature gallery: scattered spending becomes understandable statistics; those statistics become financial intelligence; intelligence becomes a realistic future plan; and the user retains ownership of the data throughout.

This approach is selected because it supports a premium product launch, makes the feature sequence easy to follow within 25-30 seconds, and matches Artami's warm, considered visual language.

### Scenario Values Used Across Scenes

- Monthly income: `Rp 5.000.000`.
- Expenses: `Rp 4.600.000`.
- Current savings: `Rp 400.000` / `8%`.
- Projected achievable savings target: `Rp 600.000` / `12%`.
- Health Score: `54 sekarang -> target 68`.
- FI Index: `12%`.
- Expense categories: Kos & Tagihan `Rp 1.840.000`; Makan & Minum `Rp 920.000`; Langganan `Rp 920.000`; Jajan `Rp 552.000`; Transportasi `Rp 368.000`.

### Output and Safe Zones

- Primary composition: `1920 x 1080`, 30 fps, H.264 MP4.
- Fallback export: `1280 x 720` for platforms with a resolution limit.
- Portrait phone is contained within the right-side 45% product region so it can become the focal surface in a separately recomposed vertical cutdown.
- Headlines and critical figures stay away from the outer frame edges, preserving room for future platform overlays and re-layout.

## Planned Sequence

### 0-3 seconds: Abstract Money Pressure

- Cream background with Artami mesh gradients.
- A restrained mix of transaction notifications, payment chips, small expense amounts, and a declining balance.
- Relatable labels appear first: `Jajan`, `Ngopi`, `Kondangan`, `Tagihan`.
- The cards and numbers resolve into structured categories: `Makan & Minum`, `Transportasi`, `Tagihan`, `Tabungan`.
- On-screen copy direction:
  - `Gaji masuk, tapi cepat habis.`
  - `Setiap bulan, uangmu ke mana aja?`
- The chaotic inputs softly snap into an Artami statistics view.

### Statistics: Organized Clarity

- Present the statistics as one escalating sequence instead of separate feature cards:
  1. Top expenses establish what consumes the budget.
  2. An expense donut shows category proportions.
  3. A daily heatmap shows when spending occurs.
  4. Savings rate condenses the data into a positive progress signal.
  5. Cash-flow projection extends the trend into the future.
  6. Health Score and FI Index complete the intelligence story.
- Narrative progression: **what happened -> when it happened -> how well you are doing -> where you are heading**.
- Use real Artami-style cards, chart treatment, currency formatting, and category colors.
- The goal is to show that everyday spending becomes understandable, not to present a dense finance dashboard.
- All scenes use one consistent, realistic fictional financial scenario. Expense totals, category shares, savings rate, forecast, Health Score, and FI Index must be mathematically and narratively connected rather than independently optimized display values.

### Intelligence: Health, FI, and Forecast

- These are one unified financial-intelligence sequence, not independent feature advertisements.
- Health Score message direction: `Pahami kondisi keuanganmu.`
- FI Index message direction: `Lihat seberapa dekat kamu ke tujuan finansialmu.`
- Forecast message direction: `Rencanakan, dan lihat prediksi masa depanmu.`
- Motion uses gentle count-ups, progress fills, forecast-line growth, and soft camera drift.

### Google Sheets: Data Ownership

- Use a hybrid reveal.
- Start with Artami-styled cream/earth/violet transaction rows.
- Transition into a recognizable literal spreadsheet grid and a clear Google Sheets icon reveal.
- Approved message order:
  - `Data tetap milikmu.`
  - `Bukan di server kami.`
  - `Tersimpan di Google Sheets-mu.`

### Real App Proof

- Include 3-5 seconds of a real vertical Android screen recording near the end.
- This is authentic product proof, not AI-generated footage.
- Approved interaction flow: Home dashboard -> Statistics -> one intelligence view -> Home dashboard.
- Do not use the existing recording as-is: it reveals a personal name and `ultah.biz.id` in the browser URL.
- Re-record using a generic demo profile and safe fictional data. Prefer the installed Android app so a browser URL is not visible; otherwise crop only when it does not compromise the product proof.
- Never reveal personal names, email addresses, spreadsheet IDs, website URLs, or real transactions.

### End Card

- Artami icon.
- `Download Artami`.
- Official Google Play badge.
- No price, upgrade pitch, QR code, or secondary CTA.

## Audio Direction

- No narration or voiceover.
- Use subtle payment confirmations, notification/bell cues, tactile taps, swipes, card pops, and number ticks.
- Use an understated, optimistic electronic music bed plus UI sound effects.
- Use commercially safe placeholder music and SFX during production; they may be replaced later with approved final assets.
- UI sounds support transitions without overpowering the product visuals.

## Production Approach

- Build with **Remotion** as a code-generated, editable project.
- Use animated Artami UI mockups for most of the video and a short real app capture for proof.
- Avoid generative AI video for the product UI because it can distort interfaces, Indonesian copy, data, and Google Sheets visuals.
- A human operator can edit the final composition in Remotion Studio without rewriting core animation logic.
- Define pacing from the existing Artami UI rather than external motion references: calm camera drift, soft springs, gentle count-ups, and clear transitions.

## Landscape Composition

- Default landscape composition is approved: Indonesian copy and supporting animated data occupy the left 55% of the frame; a tall Artami phone/mockup occupies the right 45%.
- The phone remains a portrait product surface inside the landscape master, reinforcing that Artami is mobile-first while keeping the campaign composition cinematic.

## Editable Controls

The Remotion project should expose controls for:

- Indonesian copy and CTA text.
- Scene durations and total runtime.
- Brand colors.
- Illustrated transaction labels, amounts, and statistics.
- A single editable fictional financial scenario that drives all displayed figures.
- Health Score, FI Index, and forecast display values.
- UI mockup states and screenshots.
- Real screen-recording file path and trim.
- Music and UI sound-effect asset paths and volumes.
- Artami icon and Google Play badge assets.
- Landscape master, 720p fallback, and separately recomposed vertical output configurations.

## Manual Inputs Needed Before Final Render

1. A 3-5 second clean Artami screen recording using a generic demo profile and safe sample data; it must not reveal a personal name or browser URL.
2. Final official Google Play badge and Play Store listing URL after approval. Use the current `public/icons/icon.svg` as the Artami icon source unless a replacement is provided.
3. Confirmation that the shown Health Score, FI Index, and forecast examples accurately represent released functionality.
4. Approval of final Indonesian marketing copy.
5. Approved music and sound-effect assets with commercial-use rights.
6. Final logo/icon variant if it differs from the current Artami asset.

## Remaining Decisions

- Precise on-screen copy and scene timing.
- Landscape and vertical safe-zone specifications.
- Final sound library and music license.
- Motion references and final approval criteria.

## Decision Checklist

The remaining decisions are grouped by impact so critical choices can be answered together before lower-impact polish choices.

### Critical: Required Before Storyboard Approval

1. Define landscape and vertical safe zones during storyboard design.

### Medium: Needed Before Final Render

1. Approve the exact Indonesian scene copy after the storyboard draft.
2. Replace placeholder music/SFX and Google Play badge with final approved assets before publication.

### Nice To Know: Can Be Decided During Polish

1. Preferred device frame for the animated mockups.
2. Whether the landscape variation should be re-composed or only cropped from the vertical-safe master.
3. Whether the end card should show the Play Store URL in small text.
4. Whether future cutdowns should emphasize statistics, financial intelligence, or data ownership first.
